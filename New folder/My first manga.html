My first manga

created ,Septemper 10,2020

I had inspiration to start writing manga that day

Shinichi is a bright and enterprising young boy who is determined to make his own fortune. He comes from a humble background, and his family struggles to make ends meet, so Shinichi takes it upon himself to earn some extra money.

He starts by selling homemade snacks at school, which quickly become popular among his classmates. Soon, Shinichi is making a tidy profit and expanding his business, using his creative skills and innovative ideas to come up with new products and marketing strategies.

But Shinichi is not content to just sell snacks at school. He wants to take his business to the next level and reach a wider audience. So he starts selling his snacks online, using social media to promote his products and reach customers from all over the country.

As his business grows, Shinichi faces new challenges and obstacles. He has to deal with competitors, manage his finances, and balance his schoolwork with his entrepreneurial pursuits. But he never gives up, and he continues to push himself to new heights of success.

Eventually, Shinichi's hard work and determination pay off, and he becomes a successful entrepreneur, admired and respected by his peers and mentors. He never forgets his humble beginnings, and he uses his wealth and influence to help others and make a positive impact in the world.
