My Funniest Manga

Created on September 30, 2021

My friends couldn't stop laughing while reading this manga. Highly recommended!

Ryo is a hardworking salaryman who loves his job, but he's always felt like something was missing in his life.

One day, he discovers a stray cat outside his office and decides to take it in. To his surprise, the cat turns out to be his boss, Mr. Nakamura, who has been transformed into a feline due to a mysterious curse!

At first, Ryo is shocked and tries to figure out how to change his boss back. But as he spends more time with Mr. Nakamura the cat, he starts to realize that his boss is actually a pretty good listener and provides him with a lot of comfort and support. In fact, Mr. Nakamura seems to have a better understanding of Ryo's job and work than ever before!

But of course, having a cat for a boss is not without its challenges. Mr. Nakamura keeps getting into all sorts of cat-like mischief around the office, from knocking over coffee cups to napping on important paperwork. Ryo tries to keep up with his boss's whims, but he soon realizes that he's not the only one who has noticed the cat's peculiar behavior.

One day, the CEO of the company comes to visit and is horrified to find a cat sitting in the boss's chair. Ryo tries to explain the situation, but the CEO doesn't believe him and threatens to fire him. Just as Ryo is about to lose his job, Mr. Nakamura suddenly transforms back into a human, revealing that the curse had finally been lifted.

As it turns out, Mr. Nakamura had been struggling with stress and overwork, and the curse had given him a chance to take a break and see things from a different perspective. With a newfound appreciation for his employees, he implements some changes around the office to make everyone's work-life balance better, including letting Ryo work from home one day a week so he can spend more time with his furry friend.

From that day on, Ryo and Mr. Nakamura have an even stronger bond, and they continue to work together to make the company a better place.
