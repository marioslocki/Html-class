Really loved this one

In a dystopian future, people are forced to participate in a deadly game for the entertainment of the wealthy elite. The game is simple: two players are placed in an arena, and the first one to kill the other wins.

Kazuo, a young man who was forced to participate in the game, finds himself face to face with his opponent, a ruthless and experienced killer. Kazuo knows he has no chance of winning in a direct confrontation, so he decides to use his wits instead.

He lures his opponent into a trap, using a decoy to distract him while Kazuo sneaks up from behind with a makeshift weapon. In a surprise attack, Kazuo strikes his opponent down and wins the game.

But just as he is about to celebrate his victory, he realizes that the audience is not pleased with his performance. They were expecting a more brutal and entertaining fight, and Kazuo's victory was too quick and anticlimactic.

In a sudden turn of events, Kazuo is now forced to fight against a new opponent, and this time, the stakes are even higher. Will Kazuo be able to survive this deadly game of death and regain his freedom?
